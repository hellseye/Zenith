AGENTS

Vira — The Mist Hashira

Role: Controller

Q– Miasma Veil: Releases a spreading purple mist that obscures enemy vision and slowly drains health.

E – Phantom Step: Teleports silently through misted areas, becoming semi-invisible for 2 seconds.

C – Toxic Bloom: Plants a trap that bursts into a corrosive gas when enemies approach, slowing and damaging them.

X (Ultimate) – Breath of the Hashira: Engulfs a wide area in a dense poisonous fog — enemies inside are blinded, slowed, and take continuous damage over time.

Kael — The Space Distortionist

Role: Initiator

Q – Warp Pulse: Launches a sphere that distorts gravity, pulling enemies slightly toward its center.

E – Phase Rift:Creates a temporary portal allowing teammates to reposition quickly through short distances.

C – Singularity Grenade: Deploys a small orb that collapses after 3 seconds, disorienting enemies.

X (Ultimate) – Event Horizon: Summons a massive gravity dome where bullets travel slower and enemies are dragged toward the middle.

Liora — The Time Weaver

Role: Sentinel

Q – Rewind: Instantly restores health and position to where she was 3 seconds ago.

E – Temporal Trap: Deploys a time field that slows enemies who enter it.

C – Chrono Mark: Marks an ally — if they’re eliminated within 5 seconds, they’re instantly revived with partial health.

X (Ultimate) – Chrono Break:Freezes time for 3 seconds in a small area; only Liora can move freely.

Elara — The Luminary

Role: Duelist / Support Hybrid

Q – Radiant Slash: Throws an energy blade that bounces between enemies, dealing moderate damage.

E – Solar Dash: Dashes forward, leaving a trail of radiant energy that heals allies who cross it.

C – Prism Barrier: Deploys a curved wall of light that blocks bullets but allows vision.

X (Ultimate) – Dawnfall:Channels solar energy to release a massive beam of light, healing allies and damaging enemies in its path.
